MultiSearch is an all-in-one search engine. It can get troublesome opening many websites to look for the same thing. Let MultiSearch do that work for you. Just type the name of any of the supported websites and type whatever you want to search. (Example: "Google what is today's weather?") You will be presented with the desired results instantly.
Supported Websites: Amazon, Bing, Docs, Flipkart, Google, Hotstar, Linkedin, Myntra, Netflix, Reddit, Sheets, Slides, Spotify, Twitter, Wikipedia & Yahoo!.
